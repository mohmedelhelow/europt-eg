Origin & Background

Founded in 2016, EuroPT is a dynamic growing Post tension services provider utilizing the Leading European post tensioning systems Mekano4(MK4).
EuroPT is the sole representative of MK4 in Egypt for pre-stressed slabs, tanks and other special engineering structures, whereas MK4 has pledged to fully backup EUROPT with its technical and commercial expertise.
EuroPT is unique in its structure as it draws on the advantages of the extensive know-how of MK4 along with the significant experience of its shareholders & management team in the Egyptian market.
